Symptoms

Small, circular water-soaked spots on older leaves, which gradually turn brown with black halos.

Causes

Fungal pathogen Septoria lycopersici, aggravated by warm weather, high moisture, and dense crop foliage.

Recommended Treatments

Organic: Apply Neem oil solution or copper fungicide sprays weekly. Remove infected residues.

Chemical: Spray Mancozeb or Chlorothalonil according to label dosage.

Prevention: Water at the base of plants, space out crops, crop rotation.
